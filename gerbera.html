Gerbera Flower Plant

  • Botanical Name: Gerbera jamesonii
  • Indian Name (Hindi): Gerbera (गेरबेरा)
  • Common Names: Gerbera Daisy, Transvaal Daisy
  • Appearance: Large, brightly colored daisy-like flowers with a central disc floret; pink, orange, red, yellow
  • Foliage: Deep green, lobed leaves forming a basal rosette
  • Height: Typically 10–18 inches
  • Family: Asteraceae
  • Origin: Native to South Africa; widely cultivated in India
  • Uses: Ornamental gardening, floral arrangements, greenhouse cultivation
  • Care: Prefers bright indirect light, well-drained soil, moderate watering; sensitive to overwatering
