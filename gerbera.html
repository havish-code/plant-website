Gerbera Flower Plant

  • Botanical Name: Gerbera jamesonii
  • Indian Name (Hindi): Gerbera (गेरबेरा)
  • Common Names: Gerbera Daisy, Transvaal Daisy
  • Appearance: Brightly colored daisy-like flowers
  • Foliage: Deep green lobed leaves
  • Height: 10–18 inches
  • Family: Asteraceae
  • Origin: South Africa
  • Uses: Decoration, bouquets, gardens
  • Care: Bright light, well-drained soil, moderate watering
