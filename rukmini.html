Rukmini Flower Plant

  • Botanical Name: Ixora coccinea
  • Indian Name (Hindi): Rukmini
  • Common Names: Jungle Flame, Flame of the Woods
  • Appearance: Star-shaped flowers in dense clusters; vibrant red, orange, or pink
  • Foliage: Glossy evergreen leaves
  • Height: Typically 4–6 feet in urban gardens
  • Family: Rubiaceae
  • Origin: Native to tropical Asia; widely cultivated in India
  • Uses: Ornamental hedges, temple gardens, landscaping
  • Care: Thrives in full sun, prefers well-drained soil, responds well to pruning, moderate watering
