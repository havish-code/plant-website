Sadabahar Flower Plant

  • Botanical Name: Catharanthus roseus
  • Indian Name (Hindi): Sadabahar
  • Common Names: Periwinkle, Madagascar Periwinkle, Rosy Periwinkle, Vinca
  • Appearance: Five-petaled pink flower with glossy green leaves; often grown in pots
  • Foliage: Glossy green leaves
  • Height: Typically 1–2 feet
  • Family: Apocynaceae
  • Origin: Native to Madagascar; widely cultivated in India
  • Uses: Ornamental gardening; medicinal (source of vincristine and vinblastine)
  • Care: Thrives in full sun, tolerates heat and poor soil, blooms year-round
