Madagascar Almond Tree

  • Botanical Name: Terminalia mantaly
  • Common Names: Madagascar Almond, Umbrella Tree
  • Appearance: Layered foliage with reddish young leaves turning green
  • Fruit Production: Does not produce almonds; ornamental only
  • Height: 10–20 feet in urban areas
  • Family: Combretaceae
  • Origin: Madagascar
  • Uses: Shade, landscaping, school gardens
  • Care: Full sunlight, well-drained soil, low to moderate watering
